Features

Everything you need to run your Telegram business

StreamFlow provides a complete suite of tools to automate posting, manage inventory, and capture customer orders directly within Telegram.

Automated Posting

Schedule your product catalog to be posted automatically to your Telegram channels at optimal times. Set it and forget it.

Interactive Shopping Cart

Every post includes interactive inline buttons, allowing customers to add items to their cart and checkout without leaving Telegram.

Built-in CRM

Automatically collect customer information, purchase history, and contact details in a centralized dashboard for future marketing.

Analytics & Insights

Track which products perform best, monitor channel growth, and understand your sales funnel with detailed analytics.

Payment Integrations

Seamlessly collect payments via Telebirr, CBE Birr, and Chapa directly integrated into the checkout flow.

Multi-Admin Access

Invite your team to help manage the store. Assign different roles and permissions for inventory management and order fulfillment.
